Common Questions

Frequently
Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your consultation.

Does Botox hurt? +
Most patients report only mild, brief discomfort similar to a tiny pinch. Ultra-fine needles are used and the procedure takes only a few minutes.
When will I see results? +
Initial effects are visible within 3–5 days, with full results appearing after 7–14 days.
How long do results last? +
Results generally last between 3–6 months depending on treatment area and individual factors.
Will I look frozen or unnatural? +
No. Our philosophy focuses on natural facial harmony and subtle enhancement rather than overcorrection.
